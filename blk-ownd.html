What Is BLK OWND

One Platform. Two Sides. One Community.

BLK OWND is a two-sided marketplace connecting Black-owned businesses — selling products and services across every category — with buyers who are ready and eager to support them. Whether you're a boutique owner, a freelance designer, a caterer, a personal trainer, or a licensed contractor, BLK OWND is built for you.

On the other side, buyers get a single destination to discover verified Black-owned businesses near them or across the country — making it easier than ever to put their money where their values are.

The Bigger Picture

This Is More Than a Marketplace. It's Economic Infrastructure.

Studies indicate that money spent at Black-owned businesses circulates within Black communities at a significantly higher rate than dollars spent elsewhere. BLK OWND exists to accelerate that circulation — to make it easier, faster, and more habitual for communities to invest in themselves.

Every business listed is an act of visibility. Every purchase made is an act of solidarity. Every dollar kept in the community is a building block for generational wealth.

Hillcrest Ventures Group built BLK OWND because we believe technology should do more than connect buyers and sellers — it should build the kind of economic ecosystems that last.
