Our Story

The Story Behind Hillcrest Ventures Group.

Hillcrest Ventures Group wasn't born in a boardroom. It was born from lived experience — from seeing firsthand how the gap between technology and community plays out in real people's lives, and from a deep conviction that someone needed to do something about it.

Cody Hill founded Hillcrest Ventures Group with a simple but urgent belief: that the communities most impacted by inequality are also the most full of entrepreneurial energy, creativity, and ambition — and that the right technology, built the right way, could unlock that potential at scale.

The tech industry has long promised to change the world. Hillcrest Ventures Group is here to make sure that promise includes everyone.

The Gap We Saw

Black entrepreneurs have always built. They've always innovated, hustled, and created — often with fewer resources, less access, and less visibility than their counterparts. What was missing wasn't talent or drive. What was missing was infrastructure. Platforms built specifically for them. Technology designed with their success as the goal, not an afterthought.

That's the gap Hillcrest Ventures Group was founded to close.

What We Did About It

With a background in technology and engineering, Cody Hill knew that building the right solution meant more than just launching an app. It meant building a company with the vision, the values, and the technical excellence to create platforms that would genuinely move the needle — and stand the test of time.

Hillcrest Ventures Group was founded to be that company.

Cody Hill Founder & CEO, Hillcrest Ventures Group
Meet the Founder

Cody Hill.

Cody Hill is an engineer, entrepreneur, and builder who has spent his career using technology to solve problems that actually matter.

That clarity became the foundation for Hillcrest Ventures Group.

Cody launched the company with a dual mandate — to build technology that competes at the highest level, and to direct that technology toward communities and markets that have been consistently overlooked by the mainstream tech industry. The result is a venture group with both the technical capability and the cultural conviction to build platforms that last.

"I've spent my career building technology. And what became clear to me — over and over again — was that the people who most needed great technology were the least likely to have it built for them. That's not acceptable. Hillcrest Ventures Group exists to change that."

Cody Hill on why Hillcrest Ventures Group exists.

Our Mission

Our Mission.

To build impactful technology that fuels modern innovation — creating platforms that expand access, circulate opportunity, and raise the standard of what technology can do for communities that deserve the best.

Every decision we make at Hillcrest Ventures Group runs through this mission. Every platform we build, every feature we ship, every partnership we pursue — it all comes back to the same question: does this move people forward? If the answer is yes, we build it. If not, we go back to the drawing board.

Our Values

What We Stand For.

Hillcrest Ventures Group is built on three values that aren't just words on a wall — they're the filter through which every product decision, every hire, and every strategic move gets made.

△

Innovation

We don't follow the playbook — we write new ones. Hillcrest Ventures Group is committed to building technology that pushes what's possible, challenges assumptions, and finds smarter solutions to problems that have been accepted as unsolvable. Innovation isn't a buzzword here. It's a daily practice.

○

Equity

Technology that isn't accessible to everyone isn't as good as it claims to be. Equity is at the core of every platform Hillcrest Ventures Group builds — in who we build for, how we design, and what success actually means to us. We measure our impact not just in revenue, but in doors opened.

◆

Excellence

Purpose without quality is just intention. Hillcrest Ventures Group holds every platform we ship to the highest technical and design standard — because the communities we build for deserve nothing less than the best. We don't cut corners. We don't ship halfway. We build it right.

How We Build

Our Approach.

Great technology doesn't happen by accident. At Hillcrest Ventures Group, every platform we create goes through the same deliberate process — one built on listening, technical rigor, and an unwillingness to settle for good enough.

Step One

We Start With the Community.

Before we write a single line of code, we ask questions. Who is this for? What do they actually need? What has failed them before, and why? The answers shape everything — from the product architecture to the user experience to the business model. We don't assume. We ask.

Step Two

We Build for Scale From Day One.

Hillcrest Ventures Group platforms are engineered to grow. That means building on modern, reliable infrastructure from the start — not retrofitting scalability after the fact. Every platform we ship is ready for the communities it's meant to serve, at the size those communities can become.

Step Three

We Measure What Matters.

Lines of code and download numbers are easy to count. What Hillcrest Ventures Group measures is harder — and more important. How many businesses got discovered? How many dollars stayed in the community? How many people got access to something they didn't have before? That's what we optimize for.

Why Now

Why Hillcrest Ventures Group. Why Now.

The conversation around equity in technology has never been louder. But conversation without action is just noise. Hillcrest Ventures Group exists to turn that conversation into infrastructure — into platforms that are live, functional, and making a measurable difference in people's lives.

The tools exist. The talent exists. The demand absolutely exists. What has been missing is a technology company with the vision to build specifically for these communities, and the technical excellence to do it right.

That company is Hillcrest Ventures Group. And the time is now.
