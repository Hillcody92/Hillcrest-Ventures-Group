BLK OWND

The marketplace where Black-owned businesses get the platform they deserve.

Buy Black. Sell Bold. Build Wealth.

For Buyers

Finding and supporting Black-owned businesses shouldn't require a Google search and a prayer. BLK OWND is a dedicated marketplace that makes it effortless to discover, shop, and consistently support Black-owned brands — across categories, across the country.

For Sellers

If you're a Black-owned business, you've built something worth finding. BLK OWND gives you a storefront, a community, and a platform engineered to help you grow — without the algorithm bias, the gatekeeping, or the invisible tax of building on someone else's platform.

The Bigger Picture

Every dollar spent on BLK OWND is a dollar that stays in the community. We built this marketplace because economic power starts with economic circulation — and Black communities deserve a platform that's built with that truth at its foundation.

What BLK OWND Offers

Built for Every Side of the Marketplace.

○

For Buyers

Discover thousands of Black-owned businesses across categories — fashion, food, wellness, home, services, and more. Search by location, category, or vibe. Shop with confidence knowing every business on the platform is verified.

△

For Sellers

List your business, showcase your products or services, and reach a community of buyers who are actively looking to support you. Built-in tools to manage your storefront, track performance, and grow your presence.

◆

For the Community

BLK OWND isn't just a marketplace — it's a movement. Every transaction is a vote for a more equitable economy. Every business listed is a story worth telling.

Our Approach

We Don't Build Fast. We Build Right.

There's no shortage of apps in the world. What's scarce is technology that's genuinely designed for the people using it — not just optimized for engagement metrics and investor decks. Every Hillcrest Ventures Group platform goes through the same filter: does this create real value for real people? If the answer isn't a clear yes, we go back to the drawing board.

●

Community-Informed

We don't assume we know what a community needs. We ask, we listen, and we build accordingly. Hillcrest Ventures Group platforms are shaped by the people they're meant to serve — from the first wireframe to the final release.

●

Technically Uncompromising

Purpose-driven doesn't mean corners-cut. Hillcrest Ventures Group platforms are built on modern infrastructure, designed for scale, and held to the same technical standard as any Silicon Valley product — because our users deserve nothing less.

●

Built for the Long Game

We're not chasing trends or building for acquisition. Hillcrest Ventures Group platforms are designed to last — to grow with their communities and compound in value over time.
